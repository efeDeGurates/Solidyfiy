Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

  • Username, email address, and password (passwords are securely hashed)
  • Uploaded 3D models, images, and descriptions
  • Forum posts, comments, and likes

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To enable sharing and managing your 3D models
  • To support community features like forums
  • To maintain, improve, and troubleshoot our service

Data Storage & Security

We store your data on secure servers. All communication is encrypted via HTTPS. Passwords are stored using secure hashing algorithms.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for session management. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may only share it with infrastructure providers essential for operating the service.

Your Rights

  • Access and update your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Restrict or object to data processing

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be announced on the website.
